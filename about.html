✦ The Hybrid Profile

The analyst
with an eye for design.

Chloe Wade is a rare kind of marketing professional. Trained as a fashion designer at Parsons School of Design, she crossed disciplines to become a marketer and analyst who doesn't just run campaigns — she builds systems, reads data, and designs the story.

She doesn't believe the creative brain and the analytical brain are opposites. She's proof they're not. At every role, she's brought both to the table: building WIP dashboards alongside lookbooks, optimizing ad spend alongside color stories, leading teams alongside leading briefs.

Currently completing her MSc in Digital Marketing & Analytics at TBS Education in Barcelona (May 2026), she's deepening her quantitative skills while never losing the design intuition that makes her campaigns feel as good as they perform.
